Refund Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

1. Refund Eligibility

We want you to be 100% satisfied with your learning experience. If you are not satisfied with a paid course, you can request a full refund within 30 days of the purchase date, provided that you have watched less than 20% of the course content.

2. Non-Refundable Items

Refunds are not available for courses where a certificate has already been issued, or for subscription-based plan fees (if any).

3. Request Process

To request a refund, please send an email to refunds@sikshasetu.com with your order number and the reason for the request. We aim to process all requests within 5-7 business days.

4. Method of Refund

Approved refunds will be processed back to the original method of payment used during the purchase. Please allow some additional days for the credit to appear in your account, depending on your bank.

For any billing-related inquiries, please reach out to billing@sikshasetu.com

Billing Support

Our billing team is available for any questions regarding your payments and refunds.
